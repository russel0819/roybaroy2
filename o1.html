Realizing growth, economic development, sustainability through mining

ESPRESSO MORNINGS

By Joe Zaldarriaga

September 25, 2023


The Philippines possesses vast mineral resources that could significantly boost its economy through mining. However, longstanding challenges like policy issues and environmental concerns have hindered the industry's growth. Despite this, mining has proven its resilience, providing stability during the pandemic and even increasing employment to 184,000 in 2020.

As the world's demand for green metals like nickel and lithium rises for clean technology, the Philippines has an urgent opportunity to develop its mining industry. Leaders emphasize that sustainable mining can drive economic growth, generate local employment, and contribute substantial revenue to the government through taxes. To seize these opportunities, stakeholders must work together to ensure responsible and sustainable mining practices that benefit both the present and future generations, emphasizing the importance of unity and shared benefits over divisive debates.
