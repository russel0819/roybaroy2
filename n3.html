Palace names new justices, presidential assistant

By Ruth Abbey Gita-Carlos

September 29, 2023, 6:10 pm


MANILA - Malaca√±ang has revealed the names of the new appointees chosen by President Ferdinand R. Marcos Jr. These appointments include associate justices for the Court of Appeals (CA), Court of Tax Appeals (CTA), and Sandiganbayan. The list, which was posted on the Presidential Communications Office's official Facebook page, includes Lorna Francisca Catris Cheng, Ma. Consejo Gengos-Ignalaga, and Raymond Joseph Javier as CA associate justices, Henry Angeles as CTA associate justice, and Juliet Manalo-San Gaspar as Sandiganbayan associate justice.

Additionally, President Marcos appointed former Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) chief-of-staff Gen. Andres Centino as Presidential Assistant for Maritime Concerns. Centino had previously been appointed as Presidential Adviser on the West Philippine Sea in July. He brings a wealth of military experience to the role, having served in various high-ranking positions within the AFP. Teodoro Herbosa was also reappointed as Department of Health (DOH) secretary after a delay in his nomination process due to time constraints and a high volume of questions from the Commission on Appointments. Herbosa had previously served as DOH undersecretary and as a special adviser to the National Task Force Against Covid-19 under a previous administration.
